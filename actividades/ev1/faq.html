How can we help you?

Mars Travel Packages

Each Mars Travel Package includes: A shuttle ride to Mars, accommodations in a Martian habitat, guided tours of the Martian landscape, meals, and a space suit rental. Different packages offer varying levels of experiences, from basic explorations to advanced excursions.

Travelers must be at least 18 years old to embark on the Mars journey. There is no maximum age limit, but all participants must pass a medical examination to ensure they are fit for space travel.

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your Mars travel package. However, there may be cancellation fees depending on how close the cancellation is to the departure date. Rescheduling is subject to availability and additional charges may apply.

Travel Preparations

We recommend packing light clothing, personal hygiene items, any medications you may need, and a camera to capture your journey. All essential gear, such as space suits, will be provided by Mars Travel Agency.

Yes, all travelers will need to complete a two-day training program to prepare for the journey. The program covers safety procedures, space travel protocols, and what to expect during your time on Mars.

Yes, Mars Travel Agency provides Wi-Fi access in our Martian habitats. However, please note that connection speeds may be slower than on Earth due to the distance between Mars and Earth.
